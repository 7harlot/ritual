# Privacy Policy for Ritual

  **Last updated: March 18, 2026**

  ## Overview

  Ritual is a habit tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect,
  how it's used, and your rights.

  ## Data Stored On Your Device

  The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never
  transmitted to our servers:

  - Your name (entered during onboarding)
  - Your habits and completion history
  - App preferences and settings

  This data stays on your device. We do not have access to it.

  ## Third-Party Services

  ### RevenueCat
  Ritual uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. When
  you make a purchase or restore purchases, RevenueCat processes your
  transaction data. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at
  [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

  ## Notifications

  If you enable habit reminders, Ritual sends local notifications from your
  device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

  ## Data Deletion

  To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data is
   removed when the app is uninstalled.

  ## Children's Privacy

  Ritual does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after
  changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
